About Us

Our Roots

We are a group of students who originally started meeting at local cafes to study and revise together. Over time, we discovered a shared passion for technology and programming, and decided to collaborate on developing various tools and platforms to support students like ourselves.

Our Passion

Our passion is to leverage our technical skills and creativity to build solutions that enhance the productivity, organization, and overall academic experience of students.

Our Approach

We are developing a suite of digital tools designed to help students stay organized and streamline their workflow.

United Tools Platform

Crafting the Perfect Blend of Learning Tools

Our signature project, developed with our team members CreonC and LazerTC, is a carefully curated set of digital tools designed to complement your learning journey:

  • A friendly AI study buddy, always ready to lend a hand
  • A smart note-taking app that grows with your knowledge
  • A cozy corner for storing and sharing your academic work

Like the perfect coffee blend, these tools work in harmony to create a rich, satisfying learning experience.
